The Salvation Army

What is the Salvation Army? The Salvation Army is a Christian international charitable organisation headquartered in London, England. We have a worldwide membership of over 1.7 million, comprising soldiers, officers and adherents collectively known as Salvationists. We want to bring salvation to the poor, destitute, and hungry by meeting both their physical and spiritual needs.
